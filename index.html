Leadership You Practice, Not Study

ASCEND CEO Academy is a 12-week applied executive leadership program. Participants don't sit in lectures — they serve as acting CEOs and executive leads for real organizations, making real decisions with real consequences.

  • Placed inside real startups, nonprofits, and growing businesses
  • Hold actual decision authority from Day 1
  • Supported by experienced executive mentors weekly
  • Evaluated on outcomes, not exams

The Gap Between MBA and the CEO Chair

Traditional programs teach frameworks, cases, and analysis. None of that prepares you for the moment you're sitting across from a struggling team member, an unhappy investor, or a decision that has no right answer.

  • Case studies don't build judgment — experience does
  • Most executives get their first real leadership experience too late
  • Simulated authority teaches nothing about accountability
  • ASCEND closes the gap with structured, real-world exposure

Three Phases, One Transformation

The program moves participants from preparation through active leadership to final reflection — with a consistent weekly rhythm that converts daily experience into lasting skill.

  • Week 1 — Bootcamp: Mindset reset, team formation, placement prep
  • Weeks 2–11 — CEO Placement: Lead your organization. Reflect every Friday. Meet your mentor every Monday.
  • Week 12 — Closing: Executive portfolio, final presentation, program debrief

Six CEO Competencies, Built Through Action

Participants are coached and evaluated across the six capabilities that define effective executive leadership — none of which can be learned from a textbook alone.

  • Strategic thinking & resource allocation
  • Decision-making under uncertainty
  • Organizational leadership & team building
  • Executive communication & presence
  • Operational awareness & financial literacy
  • Self-leadership, reflection & coachability

Every Participant Has an Expert in Their Corner

ASCEND pairs every participant with an experienced executive mentor — and backs that with on-demand Office Hours access to a full team of specialists covering funding, legal, HR, AI, government contracting, and more.

  • Weekly one-on-one sessions with a dedicated executive mentor
  • On-demand Office Hours with specialists across 12+ disciplines
