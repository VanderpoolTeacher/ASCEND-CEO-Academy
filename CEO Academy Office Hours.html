Your Participant Benefit

Expert Access, Included in the Program

Office Hours are built into the ASCEND CEO Academy experience. As a participant, you have ongoing access to a team of seasoned practitioners who can help you work through real challenges in real time — no extra fees, no consulting contracts.

1
📌

Identify Your Challenge

Bring a real question from your CEO placement — funding, regulation, a people problem, a strategic decision. The more specific, the better the session.

2
🗓️

Book a Session

Work through your actual challenge with someone who has been there. This is separate from your weekly mentor — it's on-demand expert access.

Apply what you learn in your placement. Use Office Hours throughout the program — whenever a challenge needs outside expertise, not just at the start.

Getting Started

How We're Launching

We're starting with the first ASCEND CEO Academy cohort — keeping the pilot focused, learning what participants actually need, and building the model from real experience before scaling it.

🚀

Cohort 1 Pilot

The pilot is exclusive to the first ASCEND CEO Academy cohort. Participants get full Office Hours access from Week 1 of their placement. We'll track which topics come up most, which experts get used, and where the gaps are — then use that to sharpen the offering for every cohort after.

Rollout Plan

Four Steps to Full Launch

1

Activate Office Hours for Cohort 1

All Cohort 1 participants get access to the full expert team from Day 1 of their placement. Participants are encouraged to bring questions early — not wait until they're already in crisis.

2

Track Usage and Patterns

Log which topics come up, which experts are in highest demand, and where participants feel underserved. This data drives decisions about who to recruit next and what gaps to fill.

3

Recruit Specialists Based on Real Demand

After the first few weeks of sessions, we'll know where the real gaps are. We build the specialist bench around actual participant need — not assumptions made before the program started.

4

Formalize as a Permanent Program Feature

After Cohort 1, Office Hours becomes a standard, documented benefit for all future participants — with a refined expert roster, a clear booking process, and session notes built into the program record.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions founders ask most before their first session.

Is Office Hours access included in my enrollment? ▾
Yes — Office Hours is a core benefit of being a ASCEND CEO Academy participant. You don't pay separately for sessions. It's part of what the program provides to support you throughout your 12-week placement.
What should I bring to a session? ▾
Come with a specific question or challenge — not a general topic. The more specific you can be ("I'm trying to decide whether to raise a SAFE or convertible note and I have two investor conversations next week"), the more useful the session will be. You can share documents, decks, or links in advance.
Can I meet with more than one expert? ▾
Yes — many founders benefit from booking multiple experts across a growth phase. For example, you might work with our AI specialist on automation, our funding expert on your raise strategy, and our marketing advisor on your go-to-market — all in the same month.
Are sessions confidential? ▾
Yes. What you share in Office Hours stays between you and your expert. We may share anonymized, non-identifying themes (e.g., "Many founders are struggling with AI adoption") to improve our programming — never specific details about your business.
What if my question is outside the listed topics? ▾
Bring it anyway. Our team has broad experience and can usually either help directly or connect you with the right expert in our network. If a topic comes up repeatedly across multiple founders, we'll build it into our official offerings.
How is Office Hours different from my weekly mentor meeting? ▾
Your weekly mentor follows your overall leadership development arc — they know your placement, your growth, and your patterns over time. Office Hours is different: it's on-demand, topic-specific expert access. If you hit a specific funding question, a regulatory snag, or need UX feedback on a product decision, you book the right specialist for that problem — without waiting for Monday.
What happens after a session? ▾
You'll receive brief session notes summarizing key takeaways and recommended next steps. For ongoing challenges, you can book follow-up sessions or transition to a monthly retainer for sustained support.
